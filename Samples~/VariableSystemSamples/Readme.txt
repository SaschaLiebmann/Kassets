Kassets Variable Samples.

In this sample, the usage of Variable is demonstrated.

VariableBasics.scene

1. This sample focus on FloatVariable instance named HealthVariable and MaxHealthVariable.
2. There are two squares to represent Healing or Damaging, which will increase or decrease health respectively.
3. Hover over Green square (Heal) to increase HealthVariable's value.
4. Hover over Red square (Damage) to decrease HealthVariable's value.
5. A Health Bar and Health Display Text will listen to HealthVariable's value change event and adjust accordingly.
